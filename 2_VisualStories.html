Visual Stories

Stories about gender inequality around the world

Each story represents real examples of gender inequality. Read through them to understand the challenges people face every day.

Girls and Education

In some places, like Afghanistan, girls are not allowed to attend school, limiting their future opportunities. Education should be a basic right for everyone.

The Gender Pay Gap

Women are often paid less than men for the same job. Equal work deserves equal pay.

Stereotypes in Advertising

Women are often shown doing household chores, reinforcing stereotypes. Household work should be shared equally.

NBA vs WNBA

Women’s sports receive less funding, attention, and media coverage despite equal effort and talent.

Toys and Gender Stereotypes

Children are often guided toward toys based on gender, which can limit interests and creativity.
