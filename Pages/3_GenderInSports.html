Gender Inequality in Sports

What is Gender Inequality in Sports? How does it affect athletes?

What is it?

Gender inequality in sports refers to barriers that prevent athletes from reaching their full potential due to their gender. It leads to disparities in opportunities, funding, and recognition between male and female athletes.

For example, female athletes often receive significantly lower pay compared to male athletes, as well as less sponsorship, media coverage, and fewer leadership opportunities in sports organizations. These inequalities limit growth and visibility for women in athletics.
