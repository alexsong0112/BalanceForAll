Gender Inequality in Sports

Lets explore how gender inequality has shaped the world of sports, and how it effects athletes. First, what is gender inequality in sports? We can see gender inequality in sports through lack of funding, promotion and sponsorship of women in sports, regardless of their athletic ability. Even women in top leagues are receiving lower wages than men in the same sport. This comes from lack of media coverage, which is why women are underrepresented in many sports. Moreover, women lack leadership roles too, like head coaches and other larger positions with authority like a board member.
