Gender Inequality in Sports

What is Gender Inequality in Sports? How Does it Affect Athletes?

Sports should be fair for everyone, but gender inequality still exists in many areas such as pay, funding, media coverage, and leadership opportunities.

What is it?

Gender inequality in sports refers to barriers that prevent athletes from reaching their full potential because of their gender. This leads to unequal treatment in pay, sponsorships, funding, media attention, and leadership roles. Female athletes are often the most affected, even when they perform at the same level as men.
