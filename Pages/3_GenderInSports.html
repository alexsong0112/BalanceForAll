Gender Inequality in Sports

What is Gender Inequality in Sports? How Does it Effect Athletes?

What is it?

Now let's look into what gender inequality looks like in sports. Gender inequality in sports is when there are 'barriers' that prevent an athlete from reaching their full potential in their sports due to their gender. This results in disparity in athletics, and it occurs in women and girls, for many reasons. For example, female athletes receive significantly less pay than their male counterpart, as well as less funding, sponsorships, media coverage, and hold less leadership and decision-making roles.
