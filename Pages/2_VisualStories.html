Visual Stories

Stories about gender inequality around the world

Each one of these stories represents real examples of gender inequality. Read through them to better understand the challenges people face every day.

Girls and Education

In some places, like Afghanistan, girls are not allowed to attend school. This limits their future opportunities and access to education, even though education should be a basic right for everyone regardless of gender.

The Gender Pay Gap

Many women are still paid less than men for doing the same job. This unfair difference in wages is known as the gender pay gap. Equal work should always mean equal pay.

Stereotypes in Advertising

Advertisements often show women doing household chores, reinforcing the idea that these roles belong only to women. In reality, responsibilities at home should be shared equally among everyone.

NBA vs WNBA

There is a large difference in revenue and attention between the NBA and WNBA. Despite equal effort and talent, women’s basketball receives less funding and media coverage, showing inequality in sports.

Toys and Gender Stereotypes

Children are often guided toward certain toys based on gender. These early stereotypes can limit interests and shape beliefs about what boys and girls are “supposed” to like.
