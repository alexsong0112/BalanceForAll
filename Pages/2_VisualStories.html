Visual Stories

Stories about gender inequality around the world

Each one of these stories below represent many different examples of gender inequality. Read through them to get a better understanding of the many challenges people face every day.

Girls and Education

In some places, like Afghanistan, girls are strictly not allowed to attend schools. This means they have to miss out on education, opportunities, restricting their future. Education should be a basic right for everyone, regardless of gender.

The Gender Pay Gap

Even today, many women around the world are paid less for doing the same job as a man. This is known as the gender pay gap where men are paid for than women for doing the same job. Equal work deserves equal pay.

Stereotypes in Advertising

In many advertisements, women are often the ones depicted doing household tasks like cleaning, doing laundry, or washing dishes. These repeated images can create the idea that these roles belong only to women. In reality, household tasks should be split between everyone, especially when more women are starting to work now.

NBA vs WNBA

This graph shows the large difference in revenue between the NBA and the WNBA. Even though women's basketball players train just as hard, they receive far less funding, attention, and media coverage. This clearly shows the ongoing inequality in sports and the need for more equal support.

Toys and Gender Stereotypes

From a young age, children are often encouraged to play with different types of toys based on their gender. For example, girls are given dolls while boys are given cars or action toys. With young children growing up with these early stereotypes, it can influence their interests and limit what children feel they are "allowed" to enjoy.
