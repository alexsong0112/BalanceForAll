Gender Bias and Workforce

How Does Gender Affect the Workplace?

Gender Pay Gap

Did you know that women are paid only 87% of what men are paid?

That means for every dollar a man earns, a woman doing the same job earns only 87 cents.

The "Broken Rung" Penalty

For every 100 men promoted to manager, only 93 women are promoted. This creates fewer opportunities for women to advance into leadership roles.

Hiring Bias

Women are more likely to experience bias during hiring and evaluation processes, often being judged as less competent due to stereotypes.

Leadership Gap

Women hold only about 29% of leadership roles globally, and even fewer in industries like engineering and manufacturing.

Harassment and Bias

Many women still experience harassment or unfair treatment at work. Studies show a significant percentage report workplace harassment within recent years.
