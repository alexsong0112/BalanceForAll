Join the Change

Be part of a more equal future

Gender inequality still exists in many areas of life, from education to the workplace. Real change starts with everyday actions from individuals like you.

Why It Matters

Around the world, many people still face unequal opportunities and expectations based on gender. For example, women earn about 87% of what men earn for similar work. Equality is not only about fairness—it strengthens communities and improves society for everyone.

How You Can Make a Difference

At school: Include others, challenge stereotypes, and treat everyone with respect.

Online: Share positive messages and avoid spreading harmful stereotypes.

In everyday life: Speak up against unfair treatment and support equality in your community.

Be Part of Something Bigger

Organizations like UN Women, HeForShe, and Girls Who Code are working globally to promote equality. You can support, learn from, and join movements like these to help create real change.

Think & Act

What is one action you can take today to support equality? Change begins with awareness—and grows through consistent action.
