Join the Change

Be part of a more equal future

Gender inequality still exists in many areas of life, from education to the workplace. Real change starts with everyday actions from individuals like you.

Why It Matters

Around the world, many people still face unequal opportunities and expectations based on gender. For example, women earn about 87% of what men earn for similar work. Equality is not just about fairness—it helps create stronger communities and better outcomes for everyone.

How You Can Make a Difference

At school: Include others, challenge stereotypes, and treat everyone with respect to create a more positive environment.

Online: What you say and share matters. Promote positive messages and avoid spreading harmful stereotypes.

In everyday life: Speak up against unfair treatment and encourage equality at home and in your community.

Be Part of Something Bigger

Global organizations are already working toward equality. UN Women promotes gender equality worldwide, HeForShe encourages collective action, and Girls Who Code helps close the gender gap in technology. You can be part of this movement.

Think & Act

What is one action you can take today to support equality? Change does not happen all at once—it happens through consistent, everyday actions. By being aware and speaking up, you can help build a more equal world.
