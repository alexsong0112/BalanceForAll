Join the Change

Be part of a more equal future.

Gender inequality still exists in many areas of life, from education to the workplace. But change is possible, and it starts with everyday actions from people like you.

Why It Matters

Around the world, women are still paid less, given fewer opportunities, and face unfair expectations based on gender. For example, women earn about 87% of what men earn for similar work. Equality is not just fairness, it improves society for everyone.

How You Can Make a Difference

At school, including everyone, challenging stereotypes, and treating people equally helps create a more respectful environment.

Online, the way you speak and what you share matters. Supporting positive messages and avoiding harmful stereotypes helps spread awareness.

In everyday life, speaking up against unfair treatment and encouraging equality at home or in your community can make a real difference over time.

Be Part of Something Bigger

Organizations like UN Women work globally to promote equality, HeForShe encourages everyone to take action, and Girls Who Code helps close the gender gap in technology.

Think & Act

What is one thing you can do today to promote equality? How can you challenge stereotypes in your own life? Change doesn’t happen all at once, it happens through everyday actions. By being aware and speaking up, you can help build a more equal world.
