What effect does linking Gender Equality to other SDGs have?

Nowadays, global issue of Gender Equality is crucial for daily lives of all human beings. Linking UN SDG number 5 to other SDGs creates a powerful ripple effect, because improving gender equality helps accelerate progress in areas like education, health, economic growth, and poverty reduction; when women and girls have equal access to opportunities and resources, communities become healthier, more productive, and more inclusive, making overall development faster and more sustainable.
