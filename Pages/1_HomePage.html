Understanding fairness, rights, and opportunities

Gender equality means that everyone, regardless of gender, has the same rights, opportunities, and respect. However, inequality still exists in areas like sports, education, and the workplace.

Why It Matters

Many people still experience unfair treatment based on gender. This can include differences in pay, fewer opportunities, or stereotypes that limit what people are expected to do. By learning about these issues, we can better understand how to challenge them and create change.
