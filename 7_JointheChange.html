Join the Change

Be part of a more equal future

Gender inequality still exists in many areas of life, from education to the workplace. Real change starts with everyday actions from individuals like you.

Why It Matters

Around the world, many people still face unequal opportunities and expectations based on gender. For example, women earn about 87% of what men earn for similar work. Equality improves society for everyone.

How You Can Make a Difference

At School

Be inclusive and respect everyone.

Online

Share positive and supportive messages.

Everyday Life

Speak up against unfair treatment.

Be Part of Something Bigger

Organizations like UN Women, HeForShe, and Girls Who Code are working globally for equality. You can support and join this movement through awareness and action.

Think & Act

What is one action you can take today to support equality? Small actions build big change over time.
